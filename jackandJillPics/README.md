# Jack & Jill reference screenshots

These screenshots are internal references for the Jick & Jall redesign. They
must not be shipped as public product assets or used to reproduce Jack & Jill's
copy, claims, testimonials, pricing, branding, or recruitment data.

## Reuse

- Clear two-sided entry points.
- Product UI shown directly within marketing pages.
- Persistent agent conversation beside the active workspace.
- Living profiles generated from agent conversations.
- Inbox and pipeline organization.
- Sparse navigation and direct calls to action.

## Avoid

- Recruitment terminology and career workflows.
- Jack & Jill names, logo, user data, company data, or metrics.
- Trustpilot quotes, funding claims, or pricing claims.
- Neutral corporate styling that conflicts with `DESIGN.md`.
- Treating browser chrome visible in screenshots as part of the design.
